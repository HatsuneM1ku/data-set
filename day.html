How to use

This page shows your daily activity as a sequence of shapes, distributed across time.

  • Switch days using the DAY 1–5 tabs.
  • Drag the dot on the gradient bar to scrub through time.
  • Each shape & color represents an activity type.

Click anywhere to close.
